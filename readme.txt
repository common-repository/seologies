== Description ==
This plugin doing integration of wordpress with https://www.seologies.com

Our website  contain 2 SEO content tools:

*	Expert vocabulary Tool - The Expert Words tool will generate hundreds of words related to your topic, alongside examples of their usage in other websites. In addition, it indicates the importance of each word to help you prioritize;

*	Text Audit Tool - With the Text Audit Tool, you can check the content of your work and see what you should do to give it the highest rank possible. Just paste either the text or the URL and this tool will tell you what to do to achieve your goals.

Plugin gives to you 10 free searches per month after registration. 


== Installation ==
1. Upload `seologies.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Seologies will appear in your admin section of your site
